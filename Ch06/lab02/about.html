About Us

Wild Rescues is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and releasing local wildlife.

We help injured, neglected, and orphaned animals receive the care they need before returning to their natural environment.

  • Wildlife rescue and transport
  • Animal rehabilitation
  • Community education
  • Safe release planning
