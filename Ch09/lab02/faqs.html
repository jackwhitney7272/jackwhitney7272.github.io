Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I find an injured wild animal?

Keep a safe distance, avoid touching the animal, and contact a licensed wildlife rescue for help.

Can I keep a wild animal as a pet?

No. Wild animals need special care and should be handled by trained rescuers or rehabilitators.

How can I help Wild Rescues?

You can volunteer, donate supplies, support community education, or partner with our rescue team.
