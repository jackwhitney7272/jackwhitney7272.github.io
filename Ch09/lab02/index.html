Welcome to Wild Rescues

Wild Rescues is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and releasing local wildlife.

Our mission is to provide care for injured, neglected, and orphaned animals so they can return to safe environments whenever possible.

Rescue

We respond when animals are hurt, orphaned, or in unsafe situations.

Rehabilitate

Our volunteers help animals regain strength and prepare for release.

Release

When animals are ready, we return them to safe natural habitats.
