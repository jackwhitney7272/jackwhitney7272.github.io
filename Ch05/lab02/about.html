About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility located in Ocala, Florida.

We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife and work to release them back into the wild.

We are members of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.
