Private Lessons

We make learning music fun! Strike a Chord offers private lessons for piano, guitar, and violin. Contact us today to schedule your first lesson for free.

We provide:

  • Dedicated and professional instructors
  • No long-term contracts
  • Flexible schedules
  • Spring and winter performances
  • Summer camps

Piano Lessons

We offer piano lessons for all ages, beginning at age three. Whether you are a beginner or have been playing the piano for a while, we have a program to suit your needs. Learn new techniques, theory, harmony, and tone. Learning how to play the piano helps develop hand-eye coordination and self-confidence.

Guitar Lessons

We teach acoustic guitar, electric guitar, bass guitar, and ukulele. We offer guitar lessons for beginners, intermediate players, and advanced players. Improve your skills with weekly lessons, learn new tips from experienced instructors, and join our rock band program.

Violin Lessons

We offer violin lessons for all ages, beginning at age six. Our violin lessons include many music styles, from fiddle to classical. Our instructors will help you fine-tune your techniques, reach your musical goals, and build a customized program that fits your needs.
