About Wild Rescues

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit organization that helps injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. Our team works to protect animals and educate the public about safe wildlife care.

Animals that come through our rescue receive food, shelter, treatment, and daily care from trained staff members and volunteers.

Safe Care

We provide safe enclosures and daily support for animals with special needs.

Education

We teach the community how to respect wildlife and respond responsibly.

Compassion

Every animal receives patient care while we decide the safest next step.

We Help Many Types of Animals

  • Birds
  • Small mammals
  • Reptiles
  • Farm animals
  • Orphaned baby animals
