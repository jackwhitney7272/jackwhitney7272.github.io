Welcome to Wild Rescues

Wild Rescues is a local nonprofit organization dedicated to helping injured, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. Our mission is to rescue animals, provide proper rehabilitation, and release them back into safe environments when possible.

We also work with the community to teach safe wildlife practices and promote respect for local animals.

Call us today at (814) 555-8989 to learn how you can help.

Rescue

We respond to animals that are injured, orphaned, or in unsafe situations.

Rehabilitate

Our staff provides care, food, shelter, and medical attention during recovery.

Release

When animals are healthy enough, we release them back into appropriate habitats.
