Club Spotlight

Each month we highlight selected student clubs and their activities. This month's spotlight is on the CCSU Scandinavian Club and on the CCSU Opera. Please check back next month to see more spotlighted clubs, and please feel free to contact us if you'd like to promote your club to be in the spotlight.

CCSU Scandinavian Club

The Scandinavian Club is not just for people of Scandinavian heritage, but for all who are interested in Scandinavian culture, food, music, and dance. In fact, we combine all three at our monthly Scandi dance event, which presents Scandi dance with live music, dance instruction, and snacks.

Here's Byss-Calle's "Gathering March." Our musicians play it at the start of each event to welcome our participants.

CCSU Opera

The CCSU Opera presents one full opera per year, plus several concerts of opera selections. The club encourages musicians of all levels to join us.
